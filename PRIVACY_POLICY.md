Privacy Policy – OWASP Live Detector

OWASP Live Detector does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal or sensitive user data.

All security analysis is performed locally within the user’s browser. The extension inspects HTTP response headers, DOM structure, and network behavior only for the purpose of identifying security misconfigurations on the currently visited webpage.

No data is sent to external servers, third parties, or analytics services. No tracking, profiling, or advertising is performed.
